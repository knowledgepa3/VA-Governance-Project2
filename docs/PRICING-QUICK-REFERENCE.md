## Negotiation Guidelines

**Discounts Available:**
- Annual prepay: 20% off platform
- Multi-year: 10% additional
- Bundle 3+ instruction packs: 20% off packs
- Startup (<$5M revenue): 25% off first year

**Firm Pricing:**
- Architect hourly rate ($200/hr)
- Governance pack perpetual pricing
- Enterprise platform minimum

**Walk-Away Points:**
- Platform below $1,000/mo (devalues product)
- Implementation below $150/hr equivalent
- Unlimited support without retainer
